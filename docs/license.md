---

## What You Must Do

You must provide **proper attribution** to the original author and link to the original page.

---

## Attribution Example

A correct attribution should look similar to this:

> Story by Albert Feijao, originally published at https://www.albertfeijao.com, licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0).

## Why This Licence

The goal is to allow stories to be shared widely while ensuring that the original author receives credit.

Creative Commons licences are widely used for educational and creative content.
